Meet Marc

Marc Nienaber Brichs is a multilingual tutor with experience teaching mathematics in both English and German. Having studied in international schools across London and Barcelona, Marc combines academic excellence with cultural fluency. He graduated with top honours, earning a mathematics prize and scoring 14 points in the German International Abitur — a testament to his deep understanding and passion for the subject.

As a tutor, Marc is known for his patient, structured approach and his ability to adapt lessons to each student’s needs. His goal is to help learners build confidence, develop clarity in their thinking, and truly enjoy the process of mastering mathematics.
