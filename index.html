About Me

I am a multilingual mathematics tutor with a background shaped by the German School London, where I developed both academic excellence and cultural fluency. I graduated with top honours, earning a mathematics prize and achieving 14 points in the German International Abitur — recognition that reflects my deep commitment to the subject and the joy I find in mathematical thinking.

I now work with students across a wide range of levels in both English and German, helping them build confidence, strengthen their foundations, and develop clarity in their reasoning. My teaching style is patient, structured, and adaptable, shaped by years of working closely with learners who each bring their own goals and learning styles.

Alongside tutoring, I enjoy playing Handball having been selected in early 2025 for the GB U20s national Team.
